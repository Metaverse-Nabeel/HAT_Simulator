Read the following information:

Seven offices in a building are to be painted. The offices are numbered 1 to 7. Two offices must be painted white, two blue, two green, and one yellow. The two green offices must be next to each other. The two blue offices cannot be next to each other. The yellow office cannot be next to a white office. Office 3 must be white.

If office 2 is painted green, which of the following offices must also be painted green?

A Office 3
Your Answer
B Office 2
C Office 1
Correct
D Office 4

Explanation

The key constraint is that the two green offices must be next to each other. If Office 2 is green, the adjacent green office must be either Office 1 or Office 3.

However, we know that Office 3 must be white (given constraint). Therefore, Office 3 cannot be green.

This means the only option is Office 1, which must be the other green office to satisfy the adjacency requirement.

Answer: C (Office 1)
